About me

Fascinated by airplanes at a young age, I always dreamt of being a pilot. However my dreams quickly came crashing down when I failed my medical tests due to poor vision. That didn't deter me from having a career in Aviation. I decided to go the engineering route got a degree in Aerospace Engineering. I worked with a large agricultural company after that where I learnt just how fascinating design and big company management can be.
The design inquisitiveness extended beyond just mechanical parts which is why I'm now into web design and full stack development.
